What is ELDT Training?

Drivers who want to receive a CDL A or B, upgrade from a B to an A, or get a hazardous certification must take ELDT “theoretical” classes.

Because SBV Expedite & Logistics is an accredited FMCSA Training Provider, you will be able to complete your “theoretical” requirement by taking the course below. There is no time limit for completing any of the courses.
